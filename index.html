About Me
02-02-2024

I keep telling myself I'm going to make time for this, but I never do. I don't know what to put here, other than my thoughts and things that I just really enjoy? For now, we will see what happens I suppose.

I was listening to music the other day while washing the dishes. A specific song came on. And although I've heard it a million times, I can always relate to it to its core. This part of the lyrics goes like this:

"I'm scared to get close, I hate being alone. I long for that feeling to not feel at all. The higher I get, the lower I sink. I can't drown my demons, they know how to swim."

The thought of being close to someone does terrify me. But I do hate being alone.. For wanting to not feel anything at all, that way you don't feel so lonely in the first place. I've missed out on something that I believe could have been amazing. I've built up walls because of someone who was suppose to love me but betrayed me.. And now it's hard for me to let anybody in. These traumas caused by this person, and others even, are the demons that know how to swim. The ones that will drag me down for as long as I will let them.
